After another rotation around the sun the time is upon us once again. Join us for three days and two nights of shenanigans, music and hugs.

LOCATION

This year we have ramped up the boogies and toned down the bush, securing an actual established festival site designed for hosting 3000 people - and we have it all to ourselves!!

With this site comes proper toilets, hot showers and a beautiful dam (we can actually swim this time).

Access to the festival grounds will be from 12pm noon on Friday 20th April.

The location is a 1 hour 15 min drive from Melbourne or alternatively you can catch the train.

ARTISTS

We have procured a tantalising selection of local artists and musicians to take you on a wholesome and juicy journey.

Hard work has gone into ensuring a smooth technical, sonic and visual experience. Official festival amenities will be connected to mains power.
